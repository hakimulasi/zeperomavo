    Who Is Jailene Holton?

    Jailene Holton is the 21-year-old victim of the shooting that took place at a bar in Northeast Philadelphia on Tuesday night. She was out with her pals at the Philly Bar and Restaurant when a guy, whose name has not yet been revealed, fired at the bar from a distance.

    The guy was evicted after being tossed out of the pub following a fight with one of the bar staff and fired at least 15 rounds into the Welsh Road tavern.

    Her uncle, James Holton, reported that she rarely went out and hardly ever drank or went out. Unfortunately, her rare outing resulted in tragedy at the Philly Bar & Restaurant.

    According to authorities, 8th District police responded to several complaints of a shooting and discovered Holton with a gunshot wound to the head.

    She was taken to Jefferson-Torresdale Hospital for surgery, but she died later, according to authorities.

    Holton's uncle spoke to FOX 29 about his niece and shared his displeasure with Philadelphia's gun violence.

    "She was a nice kid. She normally worked and then came home "Holton stated. "She didn't go out much, I mean. Her friend called, she went to Top Golf, and then they came here.'

    A 21-Year-Old Shot Dead At Philly Bar In Northeast Philadelphia

    The shooting at Philly Bar in Northeast Philadelphia began after the club turned down a guy to play billiards.

    FOX 29 found that a group of three guys came into the pub and one of them wanted to play billiards. But was turned down because the pool table was being used by a billiards league.

    According to authorities, the individual proceeded to raise a commotion and was removed out of the pub with the two other males he arrived with.

    One of those shots struck Holton in the skull and killed her. The shooter left in his pickup vehicle, but authorities want to catch him on security video.

    Another man in his early thirties was injured by the shattered glass and was sent to the hospital with Jailene.
