    Roberto Duran was a Panamanian boxer born on June 16, 1951 in Guarare, Panama. He was a four-time world champion, holding titles in the lightweight, welterweight, light middleweight and middleweight divisions. He was known for his aggressive style and his ability to fight in multiple weight classes. Duran was inducted into the International Boxing Hall of Fame in 2007.
