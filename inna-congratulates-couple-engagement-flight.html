    Inna congratulates couple on their engagement during flight

    Imagine witnessing a guy proposing to his girlfriend 10,000 meters above ground, on a flight from Bucharest to Barcelona, to then be congratulated by Inna. Inna stood up and congratulated the couple on the receiver that the crew use to communicate with the passengers.

    02 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Imagine witnessing a guy proposing to his girlfriend 10,000 meters above ground, on a flight from Bucharest to Barcelona, to then be congratulated by Inna.

    Inna stood up and congratulated the couple on the receiver that the crew use to communicate with the passengers.

    She later went on to perform her song “Tu Si Eu” through the intercom, before going up to the couple and congratulating them in person, as well as have photos taken with them.

    It was firstly posted on Agentia de Presa Mondena’s Facebook Page. It later appeared on this Instagram fan page.

    https://www.instagram.com/p/BIaiBZghoV2/

    The song in question, was released as her second single from her third album “Party Never Ends”. The song is both Romanian and English, and a full English version was also released, entitled “Crazy Sexy Wild”.

    “Tu Si Eu”, literally translates to “You and I”, peaked on the Romanian Airplay 100 at number five, and spent a total of 44 weeks in the top 100.

    She recently released new single “Heaven”, and plans to follow it up with “Say It With Your Body”, both cut from the new album which will drop in the coming months.
