    Selling Sunset Season 7 release date will be in late 2023 or early 2024. Selling Sunset is a Netflix TV series that focuses on realtors who work for the Oppenheim Group, a prominent brokerage firm in Los Angeles.

    The series not only captures their professional exploits but also their personal lives. Thanks to its gorgeous cast and interesting dramas, Selling Sunset now has many fans. It has, however, been dogged by allegations that it is scripted. Is this true or false? Come with me as we delve into it.

    Is Selling Sunset Coming Back?

    Selling Sunset is indeed coming back for a new season, either in late 2023 or early 2024. Back in June 2022, just a month after the 5th season finished airing, the producers of the show confirmed that there was not just going to be a season 6 but also a season 7. Season 6 of Selling Sunset aired in May 2023, while filming started on season 7 back in March 2023. There is every possibility that filming has since wrapped up, and we can now patiently look forward to a new season.

    When Is the Next Season of Selling Sunset Coming Out?

    The next season of Selling Sunset that will come out is Season 7, and there is no official release date for it yet. What is known is that filming started on Season 7 of Selling Sunset in March 2023. It is quite likely that filming has finished now as a cast member, Christine Quinn, once revealed that they have a tight filming schedule. Given all this, it is expected that Selling Sunset Season 7 will either be released in late 2023 or early 2024.

    How Many Selling Sunset Season 7 Episodes Will There Be?

    Season 7 of Selling Sunset will likely have about 11 episodes, which is what the past two seasons had. Seasons 5 and 6 both had 11 episodes. However, it is a different ball game if we go further back in the history of the show. Seasons 1, 2, and 3 had eight episodes, while season 4 had 10 episodes.

    Is Selling Sunset Real or Fake?

    The answer to this puzzle depends on whom you are asking. There are well-known personalities who have argued that Selling Sunset is scripted or outrightly fake. The cast members of the show, however, maintain that they are actually a bunch of realtors dealing in real estate.

    Back in August 2020, Chrissy Teigen Tweeted that she was very active on the L.A. real estate scene but had never run into any of the realtors on Selling Sunset. She also stated that her agents also have a similar view.

    Also, a TikTok user named Petite Sayo has come out to claim that she listed her home with Jason Oppenheim but that on Selling Sunset, the portrayal was that she listed it with Christine Quinn, which was false as she never met Quinn. The Tik Toker also pointed out that on the show, the claim was that the owner of the house was a male plastic surgeon who was away in Europe. She pointed out that these were untrue as she was not male, nor a plastic surgeon, nor was she away in Europe at the time.

    The Oppenheim brothers, in response, have clarified that when they get a listing, they can give it to any of the realtors working for them.
