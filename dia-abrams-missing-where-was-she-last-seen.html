    The 65-year-old Lydia Abrams, often known as Dia Abrams, vanished from her Mountain Centre, California, home on June 6, 2020. Learn more about Dia Abrams’ disappearance.

    Dia had a close connection to her homeland of San Diego, California, where she was born and reared. San Diego was about a two-hour journey from Mountain Centre.

    She married Clem Abrams in 1984, and the couple welcomed two children, Crisara and Clinton, into the world.

    Clinton enjoyed his childhood in San Diego, remembering with nostalgia the lovely surroundings and comfortable temperature.

    Because of Clem and Dia’s flourishing real estate careers, the family was able to maintain their financial security and have pleasant lives with their kids.

    She mentioned that Dia had texted her that morning to ask about the window washer she used.

    Dia’s companion that day, Keith Harper, recalled their actions to Dateline. Around 2:30 p.m., he added, they had lunch together.

    Keith worked on the property after they finished eating in order to get ready for their upcoming vacation to Colorado the next day.

    Around 7:30 p.m., Keith went back home but couldn’t see Dia. He tried to call her out of concern, but she didn’t pick up.

    Her phone was charging in their bedroom when Keith entered, and he also saw that her pocketbook was still present.

    Regarding Dia’s disappearance, the Riverside Sheriff’s Office sent an email in response. Sergeant Brandi Swan wrote that the Central Homicide Unit is presently looking into the issue. There was no more information given.

    Dia’s son Clinton stated his desire for a more in-depth look into his mother’s disappearance.

    He stressed to Dateline in an email that he wanted to spread the word about his mother’s issue since it would be impossible to get real answers otherwise.

    Dia’s characteristics were also described in depth. She is characterized as having blue eyes and being 5’6 tall.

    She weighed about 135 pounds and had blonde hair at the time she vanished. Dia would be 68 years old at this time.

    Family of Dia Abrams

    Clinton Abrams quickly departed San Diego and made his way to the property after learning the news.

    He remembered arriving at the scene on Saturday by car, and on Monday the murder and missing persons departments had joined the inquiry.

    As part of the search operations, a dive team was also sent out. Keith Harper, who was with Dia when she vanished, gave an account of the active search efforts that were conducted in the days that followed her abduction.

    Friends of Dia came together in a group of 26 and spent three days looking for her. They used Dia’s canines, counting on them to identify her smell.
