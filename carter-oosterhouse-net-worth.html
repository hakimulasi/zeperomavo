    What is Carter Oosterhouse's Net Worth?

His carpentry skills proven to be very useful, as he eventually landed a part on TLC show, Trading Spaces in 2004. In 2007, Oosterhouse has his own home improvement shows, Carter Can and The Inside Job, a spin-off show to Carter Can.
