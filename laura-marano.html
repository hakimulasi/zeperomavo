    Career

    Laura began acting in the theater when she was five years old. She has performed in a number of plays at the Agoura Children’s Theatre. Laura was employed by the Stage Door Theatre. In 2007, she also appeared in Judd Apatow’s film Superbad and the short film Goldfish. She has also done voice work in animated films such as Finding Nemo, The X-Men, Ni Hao, and Ice Age 2.

Boombox had only 6 million views in its first week and was ranked 27th on Billboard. Marano signed with WarnerBros Records on May 28, 2017. Marano announced in a Q&A video on her YouTube channel in 2018 that she would be releasing music independently.

    Laura also appeared in the indie film A Sort of Homecoming. Laura appeared in the Disney Channel Original Movie Bad Hair Day in 2015. In 2019, Laura also appeared in the Netflix original film The Perfect Date.

    Lauren was named UNICEF’s Trick or Treat for UNICEF Ambassador in 2013, encouraging other children to raise money on Halloween to help other children all over the world.
