    What is Tracey Needham's Net Worth?

    Tracey Needham is an American actress who has a net worth of $4 million. Tracey Needham was born in Dallas, Texas in March 1967. She worked on the technical crew when her high school theater director said she was too tall to appear on stage. Needham moved to Los Angeles, California to study acting. Her debut role came in the television series Jake and the Fatman in 1988 and she had a recurring role on the TV series Coach from 1989 to 1990. From 1990 to 1993 Tracey starred as Paige Thatcher on the series Life Goes On. She starred as Lt. J.G. Meg Austin on JAG from 1995 to 1996 and as Ellie Jones on Total Security in 1997. From 1995 to 1997 Needham had a recurring role on the TV series VR.5 playing Samantha Bloom. From 2001 to 2003 she starred as Inspector Candace "C.D." DeLorenzo on the television series The Division. Needham has also appeared in episodes of the TV series CSI: Crime Scene Investigation, CSI: Miami, Criminal Minds, and Without a Trace. Her last role came in the 2010 film The Last Harbor. Tracey married actor Tommy Hinkley in 1995.
