    Shopping for a gift for a woman in her golden years? The main priority is making sure your gift is both useful and thoughtful. If you’re shopping for a birthday or holiday gift for a woman in her 70s, whether it’s a parent, grandparent or colleague, you want to make sure you’re buying something she will actually enjoy! Products that make their lives more comfortable or fun are always excellent ideas, but that still leaves quite a bit of ground to cover — especially as the older consumer knows what they want. Forbes published a guide to shopping habits, noting “boomers account for more than 51% of the spending in the United States.” With that in mind, it may be difficult to narrow down your search, but that’s where we come in.

    Top Gifting Categories for Women in Their 70s

    When curating our list, we specifically kept an eye out for stellar items in the below categories:

    • Comfortable and relaxing home and fashion finds
    • Handy, easy-to-use gadgets
    • Beauty buys
    • Entertaining activities
    • And more!
