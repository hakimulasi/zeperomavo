    Is Tammy Rivera Still Married?

    No, Tammy Rivera is not married. For those who don’t know Tammy Rivera was previously married to rapper Waka Flocka Flame from 2014 to 2022. In May 2014, Waka and Tammy got married at a courthouse in Atlanta.

    But things got worse for them after they got married. When they were dating, the T. Rivera designer and her boyfriend talked a lot about how to deal with her husband’s affairs.

    But Waka didn’t stop cheating after they got married, and when he did it again in 2016, Tammy chose to leave him. Waka and Tammy agreed they didn’t want to live without each other after being apart for a while.

    The “Round of Applause” rapper said in a joint interview on The Breakfast Club that he cheated on his wife to fit in with his friends. But when Tammy left, he realized he didn’t want to be without the “All These Kisses” artist.

    “Part of who I am is because of my wife,” Waka told Charlamagne Tha God, Angela Yee, and DJ Envy. “I want a family. I want the real love … I love her.” After they got back together, they renewed their vows in January 2019.

    Waka & Tammy: What the Flocka came out in the year 2020. But when they were making the show, some of Waka’s past affairs came up.

    Soon after the first season, fans noticed that the couple had stopped writing about each other on their social media accounts.

    When Page Six asked Waka about a possible breakup in September 2021, he tried to stop reports that he was cheating on his wife. But in March 2022, Tammy finally told the truth during a BET-aired Instagram Live session.

    She said that they had chosen to end their marriage. No one knows for sure when they broke up, but it is thought to be between late 2021 and early 2022.
