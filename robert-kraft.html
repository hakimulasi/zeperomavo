    Billionaire Robert Kraft is one of the flourishing businessmen around the world. He is the chief executive officer, chairman, and founder of the Kraft Group, a diversified holding company that is known for work in real estate, professional sports, and other several disciplines. 

    The billionaire owns the first esports team in New England which is Boston Uprising. He is also the owner of Major League Soccer's New England Revolution and National Football League's New England Patriots. Tom Brady is one of the greatest successes of the New England Patriots which owned by Kraft.

    Dating life with girlfriend, Dana Blumberg

    Seventy-nine years old, Billionaire, Robert is currently dating his girlfriend, Dana Blumberg who is half of his age. Dana is a professional ophthalmologist who is based at New York-Presbyterian Hospital- Columbia. She is also honored in 2013 Super Doctors Rising Stars, New York Times Magazine.

    The duo met one another for the very first time back in 2018. After a couple of drinks and dates, the pair bounded into a serious relationship. They first spotted publicly together at the French Open 2019 and since then they are seen together in various public places. Despite having so much long age gap in their relationship the pair still seems very much in love with one another.

    Dana and Robert often spend their weekend together in lavish hotels and enjoy one another companionship. The couple often uploads one another pictures on their respective social media account.

    Death of wife, Myra Hiatt

    Robert is a previously married man. He exchanged his wedding vows with wife, Myra Kraft who was an American philanthropist and daughter of the late businessman, Jacob Hiatt. The pair tied wedding knots back in June of 1963 while Myra was still studying at Brandeis University. They are parents of four sons, Jonathan A. Kraft born on 4th March 1964, Daniel A. Kraft, Joshua M. Kraft, and David H. Kraft. 

    As of now, all four sons are successful entrepreneurs and have impressive wealth owning numerous brands. Former Mr. and Mrs. Kraft enjoyed forty-seven years together and later got separated due to the death of Hiatt. On 20th July 2011 at the age of sixty-eight, Myra died due to ovarian cancer.

    Failed relationship with, actress, Ricki Noel Lander

    Robert started dating, Ricki Noel Lander just a year after his wife's death. Ricki is an American actress, designer, model, and successful entrepreneur who is forty years old. After enjoying dating life for seven years the pair got separated in 2018. The reason for their separation hasn't been public yet. 

    However, In May 2018, Lander gave birth to the child which Kraft denied for parenthood. Some of the sources have claimed that the reason for their separation was the birth of the child. As of now, Ricki is enjoying life as a single mother while, Kraft is busy spending time with his new and young girlfriend.

    Million-dollar Houses and Net worth in Billions

    Robert is an American billionaire who has a net worth of $6.9 Billion as of 2020. He is a thriving entrepreneur who has involved in various businesses. His company Kraft Group operates in 90 countries and has over $2 Billion of sales. Similarly, he owns several Boston radio stations from which he bags Millions and Millions of Dollars. 

    Much of his fortune came from the ownership of the New England Patriots football team which worth is over $4.1 Billion as of 2020. Moreover, he owns numerous houses in the United States and other countries. All of the house prices are over $1 Million in a market. In 2010 he even sold his 7,500 sq foot Cape Cod home to his oldest son, Jonathan for a price of $3.8 Million.

    Bio/Wiki

    Robert was born on 5th June 1941 in Brookline, Massachusetts. The U.S.  He was raised by father, Harry Kraft, and mother, Sarah Bryna. Further, he graduated from Columbia University.
