    Leticia Bufoni is a professional Brazilian skateboarder who came to fame after she became the winner of the World Cup Female Street Skate Championship. Currently, she is the Executive Athlete Ambassador for World Skate.

    She is also an Instagram star and a social media influencer who keeps updating her fans wherever she goes. Additionally, Bufoni is very cordial with her fans and followers.

    Leticia's Early Life 

    Leticia was born on 13th April 1993 in the State of São Paulo, Brazil to her father Jaime José da Silva, and her mother Claudete Bufoni Crud. She was raised by her parents in her hometown.

    The birth name of Bufoni is Letícia Bufoni e Silva. She spent her childhood with her sister Bruna Bufoni.

    Career Highlights

    It was the year 2007 when Bufoni appeared in her first X Games. She was just fourteen years old when she competed in Los Angeles.

    By 2022, she has won six X-Games gold medals and she competed in the Olympics too.

    Bufoni in Olympics

    The confident lady who began skating at the age of ten must-have definitely dreamt of competing in the Olympics.

    Finally, her dream came true when she got selected for the 2020 Tokyo Olympics. She represented Brazil then.

    Leticia's Sponsors 

    Moving toward her sponsors, Leticia has been sponsored by many. To name some, her sponsors are Nike SB, Red Bull, Toyota, Grizzly, and more.

    Moreover, she became the first female skater to be signed for Nike SB.

    IMDb Profile

    Apart from her last athletic career, Bufoni is also an actress. She has acted in some movies and short videos.

    More, Leticia has got an IMDb page with four credits as an actress and ten credits as herself. Some of her works in the film industry are Humanity Stoked, Greatness Code, Push, Last Call with Carson Daly, and more.

    Bufoni & Neymar

    Bufoni and the Brazilian footballer Neymar Jr. were together in Red Bull Paris Conquest. After their fans traced their pictures through their Instagram profile, the sports lover got rumored to have a relationship.

    However, Bufoni has not explained anything clearly regarding this matter.

    Net Worth

    Therefore, the street skateboarder has a total net worth of $2 million while her annual salary is over $200 thousand.
