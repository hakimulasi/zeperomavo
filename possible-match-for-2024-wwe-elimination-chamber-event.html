    Possible Match for 2024 WWE Elimination Chamber Event

    Logan Paul has been holding the coveted title of United States Champion for two months now and he retained it at the Royal Rumble Premium Live Event last month. Now it appears his next match has been revealed.

    01 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Logan Paul has been holding the coveted title of United States Champion for two months now and he retained it at the Royal Rumble Premium Live Event last month. Now it appears his next match has been revealed.

    At WWE Royal Rumble 2024, Logan Paul retained the United States Championship by defeating Kevin Owens via disqualification. This match marked Paul’s eighth match in his in-ring career and his first since winning the United States Championship by defeating Rey Mysterio at WWE Crown Jewel 2023.

    Logan Paul made it clear that Kevin Owens will never get another shot at his title. While speaking on Wrestling Observer Radio, Dave Meltzer noted that it is likely Paul and Kevin Owens will have a rematch at Elimination Chamber this month.

    “So I guess that means they are wrestling in Australia.”

    The Elimination Chamber Premium Live Event is scheduled to take place on February 24th at the Opus Stadium in Perth, Australia. With a stadium capacity of 70,000, WWE is expected to utilize 55,000 seats for the event, promising an electrifying atmosphere.

    Cody Rhodes already confirmed his status for the Premium Live Event and Grayson Waller assured fans that the event will be great one despite injury setbacks, so we’ll have to see how it will turn out.
